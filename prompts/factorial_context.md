# Factorial — Company Context

## What is Factorial

Factorial is a B2B SaaS company that builds HR software for SMEs (small and medium enterprises). Founded in Barcelona, Spain, it's one of Europe's fastest-growing startups.

## The Partners Team

The partners team works on external partnerships, integrations, channel sales, and alliances. Roles include:

- **PAE** — Partner Account Executive: manages partner relationships
- **PBD** — Partner Business Development: finds and closes new partners
- **PMM** — Partner Marketing Manager: marketing for the partner channel
- **OPS** — Operations: internal processes, tools, data
- **CRM** — CRM management and partner data
- **AI** — AI initiatives within the partners team
- **Strategy** — Strategic planning and partner program design
- **Marketing** — Content, events, campaigns for partner ecosystem

## Common topics employees post about

- Partner onboarding experiences (wins and friction)
- Integration stories (building, debugging, launching)
- Weird or difficult client/partner requests
- Cross-team collaboration (working with product, engineering, sales)
- Process improvements they figured out
- BD/sales cycle wins and lessons learned
- Conference/event takeaways
- Working at a fast-growing Barcelona startup
- Remote/hybrid work dynamics
- Tool stack and productivity hacks

## How the team communicates

- Direct, no-bullshit. Barcelona startup culture — fast-moving, informal internally.
- English is the working language for content, though the team is international.
- Self-deprecating humour is common. Nobody takes themselves too seriously.
- "We're figuring it out as we go" is the honest vibe — not polished corporate.

## What Factorial is NOT

- Not a big corp with a PR team writing posts
- Not a consultancy — it's a product company
- Not US-centric — European DNA, global ambition
